Concerts

The Society performs three concerts with the Orchestra and Chorus every year in the Haslemere Hall. An additional orchestral concert is held in St Christopher's Church, Weyhill, often featuring a soloist from within the orchestra.

Tickets are available from Society members, the Haslemere Hall Box Office or online.

Tickets

Tickets for all of our events are available online and in person from the Haslemere Hall Box Office (www.haslemerehall.co.uk) or from Society members.

Summer sessions and other events 2013

In addition to the concert season the Society holds a series of sight-reading sessions during June. We invite different guest conductors who bring various pieces for us to rehearse, concluding with a mini performance towards the end of the evening.

The Society welcomes musicians and singers who are not currently members of the Society. Guests are welcome to come and listen, for free.

The sessions take place each Monday in June from 7:30pm. More details
