News - Highlights of 2011/2012

Membership increased by 1 to 83. We again gave an orchestral concert in St Christopher's Church, Haslemere in October and three orchestral and choral concerts in the Haslemere Hall, in December, March and May, all of which were well received.

For the orchestral concert the church was full and the December concert was near capacity. The October programme consisted of Beethoven's Coriolan overture, the Cello Concerto No 1 by Saint-Saens in which Anna Hunt, the leader of our cello section, was the soloist, and Haydn's Symphony No 104.

In December we performed Schumann's Symphony no.3 ("Rhenish") and Mozart's Requiem. In March the programme included Mendelssohn's "Hebrides" Overture, the Symphony no.3 of Sibelius, and Elgar's choral work "The Music Makers".

The May concert, on a Spanish theme, consisted of the Ballet Music from "Le Cid" by Massenet, two items by De Falla, "Danse Espagnole" from "La Vida Breve" and Suite no.1 from "The Three-cornered Hat", and scenes from Bizet's opera "Carmen".

In January the orchestra recorded the music for the soundtrack of a locally-produced film "The Long Journey Home" which was nominated for an award at the Aesthetica Short Film Festival and in May the orchestra played at the Mayor of Haslemere's reception.

We held a successful "Come and Sing" day in February in St Christopher's Church when the work rehearsed and performed was the choral suite from "The Armed Man" by Karl Jenkins.

In June members and visitors enjoyed four open summer sessions, two for orchestra and two for chorus and orchestra, when a variety of music was played through under guest conductors.

During the year we enabled students of the University of Surrey and the University of Creative Arts to make recordings of the orchestra as part of their study courses.

Our Chorus Master Michael Veazey left us after the May concert and has been replaced by Christopher Hann for the 2013 season.

Rehearsals

  • All rehearsals in the Haslemere Hall except: 18th March 2013
  • Summer sessions: 3rd, 10th, 17th and 24th June 2013

Rehearsals are in Haslemere Hall on Mondays (see below for dates) at 7.30pm till about 10pm for both choir and orchestra. We have a tea/coffee break about half way through. There is normally a rehearsal during the afternoon before each concert. Occasionally the choir rehearses in the Methodist church in Weyhill when Haslemere Hall is not available. The orchestra also sometimes rehearses in St Christopher's Church, Weyhill.
