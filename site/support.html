Support us

HMS is proud of its achievements but we receive no funding from the Arts Council. We are therefore dependent on our supporters to enable us to realise our musical aspirations but the cost of promoting and staging our concerts is increasingly expensive. Please help us to attain our objectives by contributing financially.

We are committed to promoting our own concerts and engaging professional performers where necessary but frequently have costs of around £2,000 per performance, including hire of the Hall, orchestra and top class soloists. Even with a full house, ticket and programme sales do not cover expenses.

Our goals include:

  • Encouraging students to join us (choir or orchestra), by waiving almost all of our normal subscription fees.
  • Promoting participation in concerts by children at local schools. HMS members themselves pay the basic running and rehearsal costs, but depend on supporters to help maintain financial independence - and thus artistic freedom.

How can you help?

There are many ways to help the society.

Raise money for the society while shopping online

Shop Online and Raise Money for HMS at no cost to yourself!

We have registered HMS with easyfundraising.org. It's the easiest way to help raise money for the Society! If you already shop online with retailers such as Amazon, M&S, Argos, John Lewis, Comet, Vodafone, eBay, WH Smith, Waitrose, Sainsbury etc we need you to sign up for free to raise money while you shop! So how does it work?

  1. To start go onto the Easyfundraising web site then click on 'sign up' and register. Using this link will automatically establish HMS as the charity you are supporting.
  2. Each time you shop, go to the Easyfundraising website first, click on the link to the shop/business you want and make your purchase in the normal way. The retailers are easy to find as they are listed both by type and alphabetically. You pay exactly the same price as you would by going directly to the retailer but a percentage (up to 15%) is paid by the retailer to the HMS account with Easyfundraising. Your purchase is with the retailer so your normal rights are protected.

How much can you raise? Spend £100 with M&S online or Amazon and you raise £2.50 for us. £100 with WH Smith puts £2.00 in our pocket and so on. There are over 2,000 retailers on their site, and some of the donations can be as much as 15% of your purchase. Save money too! Easyfundraising is FREE to use plus you'll get access to hundreds of exclusive discounts and voucher codes, so not only will you be helping us, you'll be saving money yourself. We need your help to start donations coming in. Join now and sign up to start making a difference ... simply by shopping.

Become a Friend of HMS

HMS Friends are the vital core of an appreciative and discerning audience, and are a sociable group of like-minded music lovers.

Become involved with the Choir and Orchestra

Friends are kept in touch with HMS's plans and activities through regular newsletters from the Secretary, who is a member of the Choir.

Friends Subscription is just £20 per annum.

Benefits:

  1. Receipt of newsletter and all other relevant communications
  2. Name listed in the concert programmes
  3. Name listed on website

Joint membership is available at £35 per annum and provides all the benefits of individual membership for two people sharing a joint subscription and mailings to a single address.

Become a Patron of HMS

We are especially grateful for annual donations from our Patrons.

Patrons Subscription is £50 per annum.

Patrons enjoy the following privileges:

  1. Receipt of newsletter and all other relevant communications
  2. Name listed in the concert programmes
  3. Name listed on website.
  4. Free interval drink for all HMS concerts
  5. Invitation to post-concert parties
  6. Complimentary programme at all concerts
  7. Invitation to attend rehearsals including summer practice sessions and Society concerts
  8. Invitation to social events
  9. Patron card to validate all the above

Joint membership is available from £90pa for Patrons and provides all the benefits of individual membership for two people sharing a joint subscription and mailings to a single address

Donations and Gift Aid

HMS welcomes one-off donations and gifts.

Gift Aid

If you are a UK taxpayer, using Gift Aid means that for every pound you give, we get an extra 25p from the Inland Revenue, helping your donation go further.

If you are a higher rate taxpayer, you can claim back the difference between the basic and higher rate tax paid, reducing the net cost to you of your gift.

Example: A higher-rate taxpayer who becomes a Patron of HMS with a donation of £100 would incur a net cost of just £75 pa. see below:

Remember HMS in your Will

Why (not) leave a legacy to HMS?

If you have valued your association with HMS, whether as a singing member, orchestral member or as part of the audience, you will know how much the Society has given to you, perhaps over many years, through the magnificence, beauty and emotional power of music.

We need your help to safeguard the future of HMS and to ensure that the next generation of singers, players and audience may experience HMS as you have known and loved it.

Preparing your legacy

If you would like to leave a legacy to HMS in your Will, your best course of action is to arrange this through your own solicitor. If you prefer, the Chairman will be pleased to put you in touch with an experienced solicitor, connected to HMS, who can offer you professional assistance.

HMS is very grateful for all legacies and is happy to recognise your generosity, if you wish, in the following ways:

  • For gifts of £5,000 or more, HMS will be pleased to dedicate a concert in your memory
  • Your donation would be acknowledged in the HMS concert programme
