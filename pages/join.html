Welcome

HMS is one of the few societies to have both an orchestra and a choir. We are always pleased to welcome new members - both singers and instrumentalists. All we ask of our members is that they should be enthusiastic and committed.

New choir members are always welcome (sopranos, contraltos, tenors and basses). No audition required. Come along to any rehearsal and meet us all.

The orchestra is currently expanding and recruiting new high standard musicians. There are currently vacancies for strings: violins, brass, timpanist (instrument available), auditions may be required for wind/brass vacancies). Other instrumentalists are welcome to inquire about extra-playing and deputising opportunities.

Both choir and orchestra rehearse on Monday evenings from 7.30 to 10.00 in the Haslemere Hall, between September and May. There is normally a final rehearsal during the afternoon before each concert

The orchestra also meets for a number of summer sessions as well, working with invited conductors.

Summer Sessions 2013

In the Summer the Society runs four Summer Sessions which again are open to all. One session is with both the orchestra and chorus and the others are normally for orchestra only. This year there is an extra choir only session. Various works are sight read and performed with a guest conductor. A charge of £4 is made to cover music hire, venue and refreshments. The Society welcomes musicians and singers who are not currently members of the Society.

The sessions are in the Haslemere Hall (please use the rear entrance) from 19.30 to 22.00.

Come and Sing 2013

In the Spring, a Come and Sing event was held in St Christophers Church, Weyhill, where a Fauré's Requiem was studied under Christopher Hann as conductor with Clive Osgood accompanying. The orchestra joined the choir for the final run-through, performed to an audience of friends and family.

We anticipate 'come and sing' events in the future where both society members and others can join us in learning a new work.
