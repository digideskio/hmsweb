Singers and instrumentalists interested in joining the society are very welcome to attend a rehearsal. Membership is then by mutual agreement with the music director / chorus master. Auditions are required for wind and brass vacancies.

In the Spring, a Come and Sing event is held in St Christophers Church, Weyhill, where a choral work is studied under a guest conductor. This is then performed to an audience of friends and family. The Come and Sing is open to all. There is a charge of £13 (HMS members £10) to cover music hire, accompanist, venue and refreshments. The Society welcomes singers who are not currently members of the Society. The orchestra will join the chorus for the final run through.

Summer Sessions

In the Summer the Society runs four Summer Sessions which again are open to all. One session is with both the orchestra and chorus and the other three are normally for orchestra only. This year there is an extra chorus only session. Various works are sight read and performed with a guest conductor. A charge of £4 is made to cover music hire, venue and refreshments. The Society welcomes musicians and singers who are not currently members of the Society.
